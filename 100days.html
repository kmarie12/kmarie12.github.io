WHAT IF... you picked up a brush every day for 100 days?

Working on a computer all day, it's become more rare that I get to put pen/brush to paper (other than to sketch out ideas that end up on the computer). #The100DayProject was adapted from a grad school workshop led by Michael Beirut into a social media event coordinated by Elle Luna. For my 100 days, I picked #100DaysOfPickingUpABrush in order to get me back to my artistic roots. Some are good, some are bad, some are half-assed...but all are interesting in some way. Enjoy!
