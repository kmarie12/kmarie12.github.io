WHAT IF... you re-designed the way clients see you?

The company I currently work for needed a website face-lift. The outdated website wasn't the only issue, however. The marketing team realized that there were two kinds of clients coming to the site: those that already had affiliate programs and those that didn't. It was up to us to present SPC as the go-to place for affiliate knowledge no matter the clients' level of experience.

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

I worked with SPC's marketing director to determine that there are two groups of people that visit the site: people who already have an affiliate program, and people who don't and might not have a lot of information. We made two personas from these groups. Instead of initially brainstorming content for the homepage, we thought about the menu buttons, then the rest of the links to important content we would want people to see. Using our personas, we determined that we wanted two different portals catered to the needs of the groups we wanted to serve. We wanted to highlight things we wanted them to explore, our different services, and calls to action.
