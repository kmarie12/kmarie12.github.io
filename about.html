KELSEY ASCHENBECK


Hi. I'm Kelsey and I like to tinker with design problems.

While they can range from big to small, I believe solution-based design can benefit any project. When I want to take a break from design thinking or trying to live vicariously through Elizabeth Minchelli, (seriously Italophile over here!) you'll find me enjoying the many green spaces my homebase of Austin, Texas has to offer.
This portfolio has been going under some major construction lately. If you head on over here you'll find a more in-depth explanation of my process, some design musings, and maybe even some for-fun sketches. You can also find me at my linkedin, my behance, or just check out my resume here. For anything else, just drop me a line at kelsey.aschenbeck@gmail.com
