THINKERY

The Thinkery is the new Austin Children's Museum. I got the chance to do a variety of print and digital work for them during my time as an intern. My favorite was creating a commemorative poster for their grand opening in a new location.

Recruiting for the Revolution

While a lot of what I did for How Do You Roll? was geared towards customers, this project was about capturing the attention of potential franchisees. The brochure’s goal was to highlight HDYR’s food and detailed training process.

Designing the Revolution

The Renegades program was a “secret” organization created to cater to HDYR?’s most dedicated fans. Members received exclusive deals, gained access to new menu items, and received points based on completed tasks/assignments. For this campaign, I created facebook ads with sneaky messaging, promotional flyers, and designed welcome emails for new members.

International Sushi Day

For International Sushi Day, I created a major push on social media to spread the word about the free-roll-with-any-purchase deal. It included an instagram countdown until the big day and frequent facebook updates as HDYR? got closer and closer to reaching the goal of 1000+ rolls given away.

Never-Ending Content

Most of my job consisted of pushing out engaging social media content using the most creative means necessary. During my time there, I designed the content for special promotions and highlighted seasonal ingredients and menu items. All the photographs featured in the social media setting are my own.
