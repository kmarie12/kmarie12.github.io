ASCHENBECK FARMS

Aschenbeck Farms was in need of (literal) branding. They needed a logo that would not only adapt to multiple business ventures but also be simple enough to be formed into a cattle brand. Cattle brand have long been associated with ownership and some have even become more prolific than the ranches they represent. Because of its flexibility, the AF logo will be passed down and used for generations to come.

I started by sketching out things based on past Texas cattle brands. Traditional Mexican cattle brands are characterized by imagery, as seen in the picture above (top right). Texan cattle ranchers had trouble identifiying brands that were designed this way, so they opted for more simplified images and initials. After many designs, my dad (one of my toughest clients to date) and I decided upon the bottom right circle AF. The curved letters represent the long road that surrounds the ranch.
